Our Mission

  • Safety before speed: Pause scaling and deployment of high‑risk general models until safety and alignment are evidenced.
  • Verifiability: Promote benchmarks, red‑teaming frameworks, and open compliance reports for external auditability.
  • Collaborative governance: Co‑develop transparent, enforceable governance with researchers, industry, and the public.
  • Public interest: Prioritize societal impact, labor, and environmental costs to avoid irreversible systemic risks.

Action Roadmap

  1. Q2 2026 · Community & Consensus

    Publish the STOP AI Manifesto and a minimal governance draft; begin open discussion and sign‑on.

  2. Q3 2026 · Evaluation & Red Teaming

    Organize cross‑institution red teaming and an evaluation toolbox; produce reproducible safety report templates.

  3. Q4 2026 · Governance Rollout

    Pilot an open propose–vote–execute process and release an annual transparency report.

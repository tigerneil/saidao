Minimal Viable Governance (MVP)

In the early community stage, we adopt a simple, executable, and transparent process that can iterate over time.

Roles

  • Contributors: Submit proposals (RFDs), evaluations, or governance tools;
  • Stewards: Maintain process compliance and public records;
  • Reviewers: Provide independent review and red‑teaming for high‑impact proposals.

Proposal Lifecycle

  1. Draft: Submit a public RFD with risk and impact analysis.
  2. Discussion: ≥ 7 days of open discussion and revision.
  3. Review: Assign 2–3 reviewers for safety and compliance checks.
  4. Vote: Community vote (simple or special majority depending on type).
  5. Execution & Transparency: Publish progress and retrospectives on schedule.

Transparency

All minutes, evaluation data, and key decisions are public by default (with sensitive info anonymized).

STOP AI Manifesto

Slogan: STOP AI UNTIL WE CAN IT.

We are a decentralized collaboration focused on public safety, transparency, and fairness. Facing high‑risk general AI systems, we advocate pausing their scaling and deployment until they are controllable, verifiable, safe, and fair.

Core Principles

  1. Safety before speed: Prioritize risk assessment, alignment research, and external audits before capability expansion.
  2. Verifiable transparency: Use open benchmarks, red‑team processes, and transparency reports.
  3. Society first: Address systemic risks and labor/environmental impacts with mitigation.
  4. Open governance: Make key decisions in public, traceable governance processes.

We Call For

  • Industry self‑governance and public policy that set mandatory safety and compliance thresholds;
  • Research community efforts to build evaluation and alignment tools for external auditability;
  • Broad public participation in AI governance and social review of major decisions.

If you align with these principles, join STOP AI DAO to shape a safe, controllable, and fair AI future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why “STOP AI UNTIL WE CAN IT.”?

The slogan emphasizes: first make AI controllable, verifiable, safe, and fair—then accelerate. Until then, we advocate pausing high‑risk systems.

Will the DAO issue a token?

Not in the early stage. We prioritize governance processes and public‑goods output, and may evaluate economics later.

How can I participate?

Contribute to open evaluations, red teaming, and governance discussions; or subscribe/email to receive updates.

Is this against all AI research?

No. We object to development and deployment that poses irreversible societal risk without safety and governance safeguards.

Do you provide legal or policy advice?

We provide public discussion and technical materials, not legal advice. Participate in compliance with your jurisdiction.
